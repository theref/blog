Sovereignty is the authority to decide rules and enforce them.
The traditional definition is - A nation's right to govern itself without interference.

So energy sovereignty is about controlling the production and distribution.
This matters because centralized entities are often corrupt, incompetent, or both.
Another way to think of sovereignty is 'not requiring permission from an external authority'.

We all live at the whim of energy companies.
Why not take some of that power back? Why should I/we be so relient on them?
